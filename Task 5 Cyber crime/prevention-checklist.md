# Stay Safe Online: Checklist for College Students in India  

A quick guide to stay safe from cyber threats and online scams.

---

## 1. General Online Awareness  

- **Stay alert for phishing**  
  Don’t trust fake emails or messages.  

- **Avoid unknown links**  
  Don’t click on suspicious messages.  

- **Don’t share personal info**  
  Avoid posting private details online.  

- **Check website security**  
  Look for `https://` before entering details.  

- **Download trusted apps only**  
  Use official app stores.  

---

## 2. Account & Device Security  

- **Use strong passwords**  
  Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.  

- **Enable 2FA**  
  Add extra security to your accounts.  

- **Update devices regularly**  
  Fix security issues with updates.  

- **Log out on shared devices**  
  Always sign out after use.  

---

## 3. Safe Usage Practices  

- **Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking**  
  Use secure networks for sensitive tasks.  

- **Be careful with QR codes**  
  Verify before scanning.  

---

## 4. Financial / UPI Safety  

- **Never share UPI PIN or OTP**  
  Keep them private always.  

- **Avoid unknown payment requests**  
  Accepting sends money, not receives.  

- **Check before sending money**  
  Confirm UPI ID and name.  

---

## 5. Reporting & Help  

- **Report fraud immediately**  
  Visit: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
  Or call: 1930  

---

## ⚠️ Reminder  

Stay alert online. Verify everything and report suspicious activity.  
Prevention is the best protection.
